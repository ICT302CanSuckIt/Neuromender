Wingman:

1. Navigating the menu

To get to the wingman game click start on the main menu shown below

When you have clicked start you will be taken to a menu that will show all the exercises you have available to you so it may look different to the image shown below. Select Elbow Raise for the Wingman game.

When you have clicked on elbow raise you will be asked if you would like to be shown the tutorial. clicking yes will show you the video tutorial on how to play the game. clicking no will take you to th next step. What the screen will look like is shown below

This page is asking which level you would like to play. selecting one will change what course you will be flying during the game. at this point clicking return will take you back to the exercise selection page, this is in case you click the wrong thing and want to go back. the options are shown below.

Once you have selected the level you will be asked what speed you wish fly. The faster the speed the less time you will have to react between rings. After selecting an option the game will start. At this point clicking return will take you back to level select.  the options are shown below

2. How to Play

The aim of the game is to fly though as many rings as you can. When the game starts it will look like this

At the start of the game have your arms by your side like the picture below:

The black line is the angle your arm is at.

The blue line is the goal angle you left your arm to in order to fly though the ring.

Lift your arm like in the picture below to move the black line.

When the black line is over the blue line then your arm is at the goal angle, hold your arm there and you will fly though the ring.

At the end of the game you can choose to play the same level again by clicking try again, pick a new level by selecting level select of exit the game. the buttons are shown below:

At any point during the game you can hit escape on the keyboard to exit the game. A warning will show up telling you that it well be counted as an attempt as shown in the image below, you then hit escape again to exit or space to continue the game you are playing.
